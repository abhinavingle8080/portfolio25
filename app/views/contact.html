Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical process for working with a new client?

I start with a discovery call to understand your needs, followed by a proposal outlining scope, timeline, and cost. Once approved, I create a detailed plan and begin development with regular updates and feedback sessions.

How do you charge for your services?

I offer both project-based and hourly pricing depending on the scope and requirements. For most projects, I prefer a fixed-price model with clearly defined deliverables and milestones.

Can you work with an existing development team?

Absolutely! I have experience collaborating with in-house teams and can adapt to your existing workflows, version control systems, and coding standards to ensure seamless integration.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, I offer maintenance and support packages to ensure your application runs smoothly after launch. This includes bug fixes, security updates, and minor enhancements as needed.

What is your availability for new projects?

My availability varies throughout the year. Currently, I'm accepting new projects with start dates in the next 2-4 weeks. For urgent needs, I may be able to accommodate shorter timelines.

Do you sign NDAs or confidentiality agreements?

Yes, I'm happy to sign NDAs before discussing sensitive project details. Client confidentiality is extremely important to me, and I take it very seriously.
